Fried Rice Recipe



Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish. Fried rice is a popular component of East Asian, Southeast Asian and certain South Asian cuisines, as well as a staple national dish of Indonesia. As a homemade dish, fried rice is typically made with ingredients left over from other dishes, leading to countless variations.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (330g) of cooked rice
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp of dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of garlic chili sauce (any brand will work)
  • 3 tbsp of oil to stir fry
  • 1 tbsp of minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup of diced onion
  • 2/3 cups (80g) of diced vegetables
  • 1 cup (150g) of peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • White pepper to taste
  • A drizzle of oil to marinade the shrimp

Steps

  1. In a bowl, season the shrimp with some salt, white pepper, and a drizzle of oil. Mix well and set it aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, season the rice with soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, and Sambal chili sauce.
  3. Crack 2 eggs into the rice mixture and mix well.
  4. Place a wok on the stove and turn the heat to high until smoking hot.
  5. Drizzle in some cooking oil into the hot wok and swirl it around.
  6. Add the shrimp. Stir for just a minute.
  7. Once the shrimp starts to turn pink, add the diced onion and garlic. Keep stirring until fragrant.
  8. Add the diced vegetables and stir for a few minutes or until cooked through.
  9. Remove the shrimp and vegetables from the wok into a clean bowl.
  10. Drizzle more oil into the wok and add the rice mixture. Stir over medium heat for a few minutes. The rice will be gluey in the beginning. As the egg starts solidifying, the grains will separate naturally.
  11. When the rice becomes fluffy, introduce the shrimp and the vegetables back into the wok.
  12. Give it a final toss for a minute letting the shrimp and vegetables heat through. Serve hot.
